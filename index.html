Your guide to the scariest and best Halloween Movies

Director: John Carpenter

Genre: Horror (Slasher)



What is it about?

A cornerstone of modern horror. 15 years after 6-year-old Michael Myers murdered his older sister, he escapes from the asylum and returns to his hometown of Haddonfield, right on Halloween night. The masked, emotionless killer is hunting the terrified teenager Laurie Strode (Jamie Lee Curtis) and her friends. Their only hope lies with Dr. Sam Loomis, Michael's psychiatrist, who knows the boy is not a man, but the embodiment of pure Evil.

Why watch it?

A masterpiece of tension-building, featuring an iconic musical score and one of the horror genre's most infamous characters.

If you are looking for pure, original dread, this is a must-see!

Genre: Fantasy, Comedy, Family Film

What is it about?

300 years after Salem's three infamous witches, the Sandearson Sisters, were executed, a modern day teenager named Max accidentally resurects them with a sacred candle flame, all on Halloween.Winifred, Mary and Sarah (Bette Midler, Kathy Najimy, Sarah Jessica Parker) have only one night to secure their immortality forever before sunrise. It's up to Max, his little sister Dani, and a boy turned into a magical cat to stop the hilariously wicked trio.


Why watch it?

For a lighter, more entertaining Halloween vibe!

A colorful, humorous adventure from the '90s with lovable characters and catchy songs.

A perfect choice for the whole family!
